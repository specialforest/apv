About APV

Overview

APV is native PDF Viewer for Android Platform.

APV project page is at apv.googlecode.com.
You are welcome to report bugs at code.google.com/p/apv/issues/.
There's also a user group at groups.google.com/group/android-pdf-viewer.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • DPad Right/Left: next/previous page
  • DPad Down, Space: scroll down
  • DPad Up, Delete: scroll up
  • H, J, K, L: VI like motion keys
